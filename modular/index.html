A modular approach

From atomic layers to reusable skills. Snap modules together and let orchestration handle the rest.

Compose

Chain modules with typed interfaces: encoders, decoders, memory, and tools. Reconfigure without retraining.

Evolve

Swap strategies, fine-tune modules in isolation, and measure contribution with built-in attribution.

Deploy

Target serverless, edge, or GPU clusters with the same graph. Hot-swap modules without downtime.

Plug-in modules

Pick from a library of well-scoped capabilities and snap them together into an inference graph.
